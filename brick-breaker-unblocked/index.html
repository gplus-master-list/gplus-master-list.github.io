About Brick Breaker Unblocked

Brick Breaker Unblocked is a classic arcade game where your goal is simple: destroy every brick on the screen before the ball falls past your paddle. Move the paddle left and right using your mouse to keep the ball in play. Each brick you hit adds to your score, and clearing the entire board moves you to the next level where bricks come back faster and in tighter patterns.

The game runs entirely in your browser with no downloads or plugins required. It works on school Chromebooks, laptops, and desktop PCs equally well, making it one of the best options when you want a quick unblocked game during a free period.

How to Play

Move your mouse to slide the paddle along the bottom of the screen. Click to launch the ball. It bounces off walls, ceiling, and bricks. Keep it from reaching the bottom — you have 3 lives. Clear all bricks to reach the next level.

Tips to Score Higher

Aim for top rows first. Try to angle shots upward so the ball bounces around the top half and clears multiple rows at once.

Use the edges. When the ball travels along the side wall it can take out an entire column rapidly.

Stay centered. Keep the paddle near the center by default — moving from center to either edge is faster than crossing the full width.
