What is Bad Ice Cream?

Bad ice cream is a maze arcade game originally created by Nitrome, where you play as a walking scoop of ice cream on a mission to collect every piece of fruit on the level. The twist is that the entire arena is made of ice, and your character has a unique power: you can shoot out rows of ice blocks to build walls, and smash through existing ice to carve new paths. Enemies patrol the maze, and one touch melts your run instantly.

What made the game famous is how the ice mechanic works both ways. A wall of ice can trap an enemy and save your life, or it can accidentally seal you into a corner with no way out. Every level becomes a small puzzle about when to build and when to break.

How to Play

  • Use the arrow keys to move your ice cream around the maze.
  • Press space to shoot a line of ice blocks, or to shatter blocks already in front of you.
  • Collect every fruit on the map to finish the level. Fruit often arrives in waves, so grab the first set and a new set appears.
  • Avoid all enemies. Trolls, pixies and other creatures each move in their own pattern.

Tips for Beating More Levels

Learn enemy patterns first. Most enemies follow fixed routes or simple chase logic. Spend the first few seconds of a level just watching how they move before committing to a route.

Use ice as a shield, not just a tool. When a wave of fruit spawns in a dangerous spot, wall off the nearest enemy before collecting. A single row of blocks buys you all the time you need.

Never break ice blindly. Smashing a long line of ice removes the whole row at once, and sometimes there is an enemy waiting on the other side. Break one step at a time near unexplored areas.

In 2-player mode, split the map. The game supports two players on one keyboard, and the fastest way to clear fruit waves is dividing the level into zones instead of following each other around.
